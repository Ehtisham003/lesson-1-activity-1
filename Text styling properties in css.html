Typography with food

Food, substance consisting essentially of protein, carbohydrate, fats, and other nutrients used in the body of an organism to sustain growth and vital process and to furnish energy.
The absorption and utillization of food by the body is fundamental to nutrition and is facillated by digestion.
Plants, which convert solar energy to food by photosynthesis, are the primary food sources.
< p class="p1">Animals that feed on plants often serve as source of food for other animals.
